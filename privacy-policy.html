Privacy Policy

Background

  1. Melrose Laboratories Pty Ltd (Melrose) is committed to responsible privacy practices and to complying with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) contained in the Privacy Act.
  2. Melrose recognises and acknowledges that protecting the privacy of individuals' personal information is important and required under the Privacy Act.
  3. This Privacy Policy sets out Melrose's policies on the management of personal information including how Melrose collects personal information, the purposes for which Melrose may use and/or disclose this information, how Melrose may use and/or disclose the information Melrose collects, and to whom this information is disclosed. Additionally, this policy explains how individuals can request access to and correction of the information Melrose holds about them, how individuals can file complaints with Melrose in respect of how Melrose handles personal information held about them, and to make any related inquiry.
  4. Melrose reserves the right to (at its discretion) change or replace this Privacy Policy from time to time. Melrose will post the modified, amended or new privacy policy to its website in place of the superseded policy. At any time, the latest version of our Privacy Policy is available from our website https://www.melrosehealth.com.au.
  5. Melrose recommends that you regularly check our website to ensure that you are aware of the current version of our Privacy Policy.

Melrose acknowledges that the Privacy Principles contained in the Privacy Act is from a strict legal position applicable within Australia. Melrose however is committed to having this Privacy Policy to operate globally, except where this policy conflicts with the requirements of the local jurisdiction (in such case, Melrose will comply with the requirements of the local jurisdiction).

How does Melrose collect your personal information

  1. Melrose only collects personal information using lawful and fair means. Melrose will not collect personal information unless the information is reasonably necessary for one or more of Melrose's functions or activities.
  2. Melrose Health collects personal information in a number of ways. The most common ways Melrose collects your personal information are:
    • from publicly available sources;
    • from forms which you complete and submit to us, including online forms;
    • through mail correspondence, emails and other electronic means;
    • from job applicants and staff members;
    • from direct contact in the course of Melrose providing goods and/or services to you;
    • in the course of Melrose conducting market research, including customer satisfaction surveys;
    • in the course of answering a general inquiry regarding Melrose, the products and/or services Melrose provides;
    • directly from you when you provide it to us or our agents or contractors;
    • via our website or when you deal with us online (including through our social media pages);
    • if you are an individual contractor to Melrose Health, from your employer or recruitment agency;
    • from our related companies;
    • from credit reporting agencies;
    • Work program; from referees if you apply for a position as an Employee or Contractor with us); and
    • from consumer feedback where it is possible we would likely get name and contact details. Some consumers provide fuller information such as the condition and treatment so that Melrose can help them with product advice.
  3. Subject to the foregoing, to the extent reasonably practicable and reasonable for us to do so, Melrose only collects personal information about an individual directly from that individual. Additionally, Melrose will only collect personal information where Melrose specifically requests that information, except in circumstances where personal information is volunteered to Melrose or is otherwise supplied to Melrose or disclosed to Melrose without Melrose asking for such information.

Updates to this supplement

Melrose may from time to time change or replace this supplement and Melrose will post the modified, amended or new supplement to the Website in place of the superseded supplement. Melrose recommends that you regularly check the Website to ensure that you are aware of the current version of Melrose's privacy policy and this supplement.
